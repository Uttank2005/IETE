Guidelines for manuscript:

1. The manuscript should be prepared in single column format. The manuscript must contain the following sections: Introduction, Methodology, Findings, Conclusion, and References. Sections can be named differently, and subsections may be included.

2. The manuscript must be submitted as a Microsoft Word document. All images (illustrations, charts, photos) must be placed in the text just after the first point of reference.

3. Tables must be created using the Microsoft Word table functions and included as part of the text immediately after the first point of reference. Do not place them at the end of your manuscript.

4. All reference items must be in 8 pt font. Please use APA style for references.

5. Formatting Instructions:
---Heading 1: Times New Roman, 12 pt bold
---Heading 2: Times New Roman, 12 pt
---Heading 3: Times New Roman, 12 pt italics
---Body text: Times New Roman 11 pt

Instructions for Manuscripts

Submission Guidelines

Please carefully review and follow these detailed instructions to ensure your manuscript meets all requirements for the conference review process.

Technical Requirements

  • Language: English only, with proper grammar and spelling
  • Originality: Must be original work, not previously published
  • Plagiarism: All submissions will be checked for plagiarism (max 15% similarity allowed)
  • Figures: High resolution (300 DPI minimum), properly labeled
  • Tables: Clear formatting with appropriate captions
  • Equations: Numbered sequentially and properly formatted

Review Process

  • Double-blind peer review by at least 3 expert reviewers
  • Review criteria: Technical quality, novelty, clarity, significance
  • Notification of acceptance/rejection via email
  • Camera-ready submission required for accepted papers

Ethical Considerations

  • Research involving human subjects must have ethical approval
  • Conflicts of interest must be declared
  • All co-authors must approve the final submission
  • IEEE code of ethics must be followed
