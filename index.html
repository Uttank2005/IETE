About Department
Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

The Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering at St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology is committed to imparting quality education, fostering innovation, and developing industry-ready professionals.

The department offers a dynamic learning environment enriched with state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and a curriculum that bridges theory with practical applications in modern communication and electronics technologies.

Student Forum - Electel

Formed under the prestigious Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE), serving as a vibrant platform for workshops, seminars, competitions, and conferences.

About SVPCET
St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology
NAAC 'A' Accredited Established 2004

St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Nagpur, established in 2004 by the Nagpur Pallottine Society, is a premier institution renowned for academic excellence and strong placements.

Offering UG and PG programs in Computer, Mechanical, Electrical, and allied branches, along with research degrees, SVPCET combines cutting-edge infrastructure with industry-aligned learning to shape globally competent engineers.

About NFIET
National Conference for Future Engineers

The National Conference for Future Engineers (NFIET), organized by the Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering under its IETE forum Electel, is a multidisciplinary platform designed for UG and PG students.

The conference encourages participants to present papers and posters while promoting research, innovation, and technical excellence across diverse engineering domains. It serves as a vibrant platform for sharing knowledge, exchanging ideas, and fostering collaboration between academia and industry.
